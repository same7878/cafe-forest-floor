Our Philosophy

Stillness by the Lake

Udaipur is a city of echoes and reflections, but here on the forest floor, the world slows to a murmur. We built this sanctuary with a single purpose: to offer a space where you can disconnect from the noise and reconnect with the simple textures of life.

Every morning, as the first light filters across the water, the gentle lake breeze fills our open balcony. The stone underfoot remains cool, the aroma of freshly roasted coffee beans mingles with the scent of wet clay from our workshops, and time stretches just a little longer. It is here that you find the space to think, to craft, or simply to be.

Grounding your senses, day and night.

Atmosphere & Artistry

Tactile Meditation

We believe in the beauty of hands-on creation. Our lakeside clay workshops are led by local artisans, inviting you to shape raw clay into personal keepsakes while listening to the gentle wash of the waves.

It is a tactile meditation that grounds you in Udaipur's rich creative heritage. All materials are sourced locally, and workshops run daily on our lakeside balcony.
